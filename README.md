The escape-school is a game based on Python which is somelike the text adventure type.
The game background is about a ancient China student who studies in a Confucius private school bored in the books and always hoping to play out of the wall.
From here, you can choose each options to build your way to go outside. 
If you were caught, you have to copy the Confucius works many times. Be careful to hide from your teacher!
